# WGU Brand Glossary

A glossary of terms, acronyms, and internal vocabulary used in the WGU brand and design system.

## Brand and identity

**WGU.** Western Governors University. A fully online, competency-based, nonprofit university founded by 19 U.S. governors.

**Brand pillars.** The five strategic frames the brand operates under: Renewing Education's Promise, Personalizing Learning, Advancing Systemic Equity, Partnering for Impact, Modeling Accountability.

**Voice characteristics.** The six adjectives describing how WGU sounds: Empowering, Authentic, Inviting, Modern, Bold, Professional.

**Education for the People.** The active marketing campaign (December 2025–present). Bold, contrarian creative POV with half-tone portraits and Program Nar OT Black headlines.

## Marks

**Master mark.** Common term for the **university logo**, the owl figure plus "Western Governors University" wordmark.

**Owl icon.** The owl figure alone, no wordmark. Approved only for student, alumni, and employee audiences.

**Wordmark.** The "Western Governors University" letterforms alone, no owl. Used as a smaller, text-only mark for audiences who already know the brand.

**Academic Seal.** The formal university seal. Restricted to official documents (diplomas, transcripts, formal letters from the President).

**Sage.** WGU's costumed mascot. Distinct from the owl icon. Brand mascot, not used in formal layouts.

## Colors

**WGU Blue.** #002855. The unifying brand color, required on every asset.

**Blue Midnight.** The brand-guide name for #001731. Sometimes used interchangeably with "Midnight". Used for footers and deep gradient bases.

**Deep Blue.** #003057. Education for the People campaign-specific.

**Medium Blue.** #0070F0. CTA, link, primary interaction color.

**Sky Blue.** #46B1EF. Light accent.

**Lime Green.** #97E152. Sparingly used "pop" accent.

## Typography

**Futura PT.** WGU's primary licensed typeface. Geometric sans-serif. Used for body and headlines.

**Rocky.** Licensed serif used only for isolated infographic numerals.

**Program Nar OT Black.** Compressed display sans-serif used only for Education for the People campaign headlines.

**Jost.** Free Google Fonts substitute for Futura PT in the design system. Production work should use real Futura PT.

**Newsreader.** Free Google Fonts substitute for Rocky.

**Oswald.** Free Google Fonts substitute for Program Nar OT Black.

## Iconography

**Simple icons.** One-tone, line-based icons reserved for web UI navigation.

**Illustrated icons.** Two-tone or color-background icons used in marketing collateral.

**Lucide.** Open-source icon library used as a stand-in for Simple icons in the design system.

## Patterns

**Swirl.** Curving organic motion pattern.

**Network.** Grid of interlinked nodes.

**Comms.** Speech-bubble shapes.

**Connect.** Linked dots and lines.

**Flow.** Wave forms.

**Elevate.** Upward chevrons.

**Growth.** Branching plant motif.

**Pathways.** Layered roads or routes.

## Operational

**DAM.** Digital Asset Management. WGU's DAM is **Canto**. The source of truth for all brand assets.

**Canto.** wgu.canto.com. WGU's DAM provider.

**Brand Governance.** The team that owns brand standards, the icon library, photography approvals, and campaign visual direction. Contact: brand@groups.wgu.edu.

**brand@groups.wgu.edu.** The brand questions and approvals inbox.

## Design system

**`wgu-design`.** The Claude plugin (skill) that ships brand and design system knowledge to Claude in claude.ai, Claude Code, and Cowork.

**Design tokens.** CSS custom properties (e.g., `--wgu-blue`, `--space-4`) that encode brand decisions into reusable values.

**Cowork.** A desktop tool from Anthropic for non-developers to automate file and task management. The plugin works in Cowork mode.

**Claude Code.** Anthropic's CLI tool for agentic coding. The plugin works inside Claude Code.

## Educational

**Competency-based education (CBE).** The pedagogical model WGU uses. Students advance by demonstrating mastery of competencies rather than by earning credit hours over a semester.

**Term.** A six-month enrollment period at WGU. Students pay flat tuition per term and can complete as many courses as they can master.

**Mentor.** WGU faculty role focused on one-on-one coaching and progress support, distinct from a course instructor in a traditional university.

**Assessment.** A competency exam or performance task the student completes to advance.

## Common abbreviations

**CTA.** Call to action. The primary action a piece of UI or marketing wants the user to take.

**OOH.** Out of home. Billboards, transit, and other outdoor advertising formats.

**DAM.** Digital Asset Management.

**CBE.** Competency-based education.

**B2B.** Business-to-business. WGU's employer and workforce-partner audience.

**WCAG.** Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. WGU targets WCAG 2.1 AA at minimum.
