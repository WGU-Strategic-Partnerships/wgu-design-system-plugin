# Active Campaign: Education for the People

## Status

Active from December 2025 to present.

## Creative POV

"Saying the quiet part out loud. College has become inaccessible for many. WGU has always stood with students."

The campaign takes a deliberately direct, occasionally contrarian position on the state of higher education. It positions WGU as the option for students who have been failed or priced out of traditional pathways.

## Visual specification

| Component | Spec |
| --- | --- |
| Headline font | **Program Nar OT Black** (web substitute: **Oswald**) |
| Body font | **Futura PT Book** (web substitute: **Jost**) |
| Palette | Deep Blue `#003057`, Sky Blue `#46B1EF`, Lime Green `#97E152`, White |
| Photo treatment | Half-tone style portraits of students on brand-color backgrounds |
| Visual voice | Bold, ownable, "stops the viewer". Half-tone gives a printed, manifesto-like feel. Contrarian edge |

## Headlines

Headlines are short, declarative, and use the Program Nar OT Black face in ALL CAPS. They make a definitive statement.

Examples (paraphrased):

- "Education for the people."
- "Your time matters. Your tuition shouldn't."
- "We did not invent this fight. We just refuse to lose it."

Headlines do not hedge. They assert.

## Photography for the campaign

- Half-tone treated portraits.
- Solid brand-color backgrounds (Deep Blue, Sky Blue, Lime Green, or White).
- One subject per portrait. Eye-level or slight low angle.
- Skin tones remain warm and accurate even through the half-tone.
- Subjects look directly at the camera (rare in WGU's standard documentary style; specific to this campaign).

## What is unique to this campaign

- **Program Nar OT Black** is used only here. Not in evergreen marketing, not in product UI.
- **Half-tone portraits** are a campaign-only treatment.
- **Lime Green** appears more frequently as a punctuation color, paired with Deep Blue.

## What still applies

All other brand rules still apply during the campaign.

- WGU Blue (#002855) appears on every asset.
- Logo, owl icon, wordmark, and seal usage rules are unchanged.
- Voice characteristics (Empowering, Authentic, Inviting, Modern, Bold, Professional) still govern copy.
- No emoji. No owl puns.

## Where assets live

Campaign-specific photography, headlines, and templates live in the WGU DAM (Canto). Contact brand.wgu.edu for access. Do not produce campaign-look-alikes from third-party imagery.

## When the campaign ends

Once Education for the People sunsets, retire all campaign-specific elements (Program Nar headlines, half-tone treatment, the Deep Blue / Sky Blue / Lime palette emphasis). Future campaigns will publish their own creative POV and visual spec.
