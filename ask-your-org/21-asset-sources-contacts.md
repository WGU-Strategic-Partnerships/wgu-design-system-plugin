# WGU Asset Sources and Contacts

## Where official WGU assets live

The official source of truth for all WGU brand assets is the **WGU DAM (Canto)**. Always download from Canto. Never source assets from a webpage screenshot, a partner's website, or a previous email.

## The brand site

The official, public brand guide lives at https://www.brand.wgu.edu/. It is the canonical reference for everything in this knowledge pack. When the brand site disagrees with this knowledge pack, the brand site wins.

## Brand questions and approvals

**brand.wgu.edu**

Contact this group for:

- Logo and mark exception requests.
- Co-branded asset approval.
- Use of the Academic Seal on non-official documents.
- Custom illustration or icon requests.
- Pattern modification or new pattern requests.
- Campaign approval and visual review.

Lead time matters. Do not assume same-day turnaround.

## Brand Governance Team

The Brand Governance Team owns:

- The illustrated icon library.
- Photography approvals.
- Campaign visual direction.
- The DAM (Canto).
- Co-brand and partner mark approvals.

## Where the WGU Design System Claude plugin lives

This knowledge pack is derived from the **`wgu-design`** Claude plugin maintained by the WGU Design System team. The plugin includes:

- `colors_and_type.css`. Production-ready CSS tokens.
- `assets/logos/`. PNG copies of the master mark, owl icon, and wordmark.
- `assets/patterns/`. PNGs of all eight patterns in three colorways.
- `slides/`. Six 16:9 slide archetypes matching the Evergreen template.
- `ui_kits/marketing_site/`. Interactive React kit for the marketing surface.
- `preview/`. 23 design-system preview cards.

Bentley Folkman owns the plugin and distributes it within WGU.

## What this knowledge pack does not include

- **Licensed fonts.** Futura PT, Rocky, and Program Nar OT Black are licensed. They are not shipped here. Web substitutes (Jost, Newsreader, Oswald) are loaded from Google Fonts for prototyping only.
- **Illustrated icon library.** Not shipped. Pull from Canto.
- **Photography library.** Not shipped. Pull from Canto.
- **Academic Seal.** Not shipped outside official document templates. Request via brand.wgu.edu.
- **School-specific lockups, Craft, Juvo, Academy, Labs, and Corporate Logo.** Pull from Canto.
- **Live product source code.** This knowledge pack describes WGU's design system, not the source of wgu.edu, the student portal, or the mobile app.

## When in doubt

If a brand question does not have an obvious answer here:

1. Check the brand site at https://www.brand.wgu.edu/.
2. Search Canto for the asset.
3. Email brand.wgu.edu.

Do not guess. Brand consistency compounds.
