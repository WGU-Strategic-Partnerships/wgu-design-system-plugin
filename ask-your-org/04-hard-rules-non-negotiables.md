# WGU Hard Rules: Non-Negotiables

These rules are not preferences. They override design opinion, schedule pressure, or stakeholder requests. If a piece of work breaks one of these, it is not on-brand and should not ship.

## The eight hard rules

1. **WGU Blue (#002855) appears on every designed asset.** It is the unifying color and is non-negotiable. If you cannot find a place for #002855 in a layout, the layout is wrong.
2. **Never alter logos, the seal, icons, or patterns.** Use them exactly as supplied. No recoloring, no rotating, no stretching, no adding effects.
3. **Never tint, adjust opacity of, or eyeball any brand color.** Use the exact HEX values from the palette. Do not "make it 80% blue" or "lighten this for contrast".
4. **The wordmark always appears without the owl icon.** They are two separate marks and should never be combined into a custom lockup.
5. **The owl icon alone is only used for student, alumni, or employee audiences.** Public-facing marketing aimed at prospective students should use the full university logo.
6. **The Academic Seal is restricted to official documents.** Diplomas, transcripts, formal letters from the President. Apparel or swag uses must be cleared with Brand Governance.
7. **No owl puns. No emoji in brand collateral.** This applies to copy in any format, including internal-facing brand work.
8. **Type rules.** Use Futura PT (Arial fallback) for body type. Use Rocky for isolated infographic numerals only. Use Program Nar OT Black only for Education for the People campaign headlines.

## Why these rules exist

Brand consistency is the single biggest driver of brand recall and trust. Every time a learner sees a sloppy, off-brand piece of WGU collateral, they have a slightly less confident impression of the institution. Every time they see a tight, recognizable, on-brand piece, they trust WGU more. Compounded across millions of impressions, this is decisive.

## Approval and exceptions

Exceptions to any of these rules require **Brand Governance** approval. Contact: brand.wgu.edu.

Do not assume an exception based on past work that broke a rule. Past mistakes do not set precedent.

## Quick gut check before shipping

Before any asset goes live, verify:

- WGU Blue (#002855) is present.
- Logo, seal, icons, and patterns are unmodified.
- All brand colors are exact HEX, no tints, no opacity adjustments.
- Wordmark is not paired with the owl icon.
- Type uses the approved family for the surface.
- Copy is owl-pun-free and emoji-free.

If any of those is no, fix it before sending for review.
