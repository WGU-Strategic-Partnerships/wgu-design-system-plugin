# WGU Channel-Specific Guidance

How brand rules adapt to different channels. The voice and visual fundamentals are constant. The expression flexes by surface.

## Web (wgu.edu and other WGU.edu surfaces)

- **Type.** Futura PT (or Jost as web preview). 18px body, 26px line height.
- **Color.** Full palette available. Gradients permitted.
- **Layout.** 1200 to 1280px max width with 24 to 48px gutters.
- **Patterns.** One pattern per page section maximum.
- **Photography.** Pulled from Canto. Documentary or campaign treatment.
- **Accessibility.** WCAG 2.1 AA target.

## Student portal and product UI

- **Voice tone.** More instructional, supportive, second-person ("you").
- **Type.** Same Futura PT, 18px body. Compact size variants permitted in dense surfaces.
- **Color.** Heavier use of Light Blue and Light Grey neutrals to keep dense data readable.
- **Owl icon.** Permitted as the navigation mark (audience is students).
- **Iconography.** Simple icons (Lucide stand-in) for nav and actions.
- **Patterns.** Used sparingly. Most product surfaces are clean and flat.

## PowerPoint

- **Template.** Evergreen template (general purpose). Do not mix with the Corporate template inside a single deck.
- **Type.** Arial fallback if Futura PT cannot be embedded. Flag the substitution.
- **Color.** Use solid brand colors. Avoid web-only gradients that may not render consistently.
- **Imagery.** Embed (do not link) so the deck travels well.
- **Slides.** Use the six approved archetypes (Title, Section, Content with Stats, Quote, Comparison, Closing).

## Email

- **Type.** Arial in HTML email. Futura PT inside imagery only (as raster).
- **Color.** Limited gradient support; use solid colors.
- **Layout.** Single-column, 600 to 640px wide.
- **Imagery.** Always include alt text. Assume images may be blocked by default.
- **CTA.** Bulletproof button using table-based layout. Medium Blue background, white text.

## Social media

- **Voice.** Slightly more conversational than acquisition marketing, still professional.
- **Owl puns.** Still no.
- **Emoji.** May appear in ad-hoc social posts but are not part of the design system. Use sparingly and never in primary brand collateral.
- **Image specs (current at time of writing).**
  - Instagram square: 1080x1080.
  - Instagram story: 1080x1920.
  - Instagram reel: 1080x1920.
  - LinkedIn post: 1200x627.
  - LinkedIn carousel: 1080x1080.
  - Facebook post: 1200x630.
  - X post: 1600x900.
- **Type.** Futura PT (or rendered substitute). Headlines large enough to read on a feed glance.
- **Logo placement.** Lower-corner watermark on most assets, never at smaller than 52px.

## Out-of-home (OOH)

- **Copy length.** 7 words maximum.
- **Type.** Futura PT Heavy or Program Nar OT Black (campaign).
- **Photography.** High contrast, simple composition, designed for distance reading.
- **Color.** Bold blocking. WGU Blue, Medium Blue, or campaign palette.
- **Logo.** Included on every OOH placement. Minimum 0.725 inch print width applies.

## Video

- **Open with brand.** University logo within the first 2 seconds or as a quick stinger.
- **Lower thirds.** Futura PT Medium for name, Futura PT Book Italic for role and program.
- **Captions.** Always provide. Sentence case. Burned-in or sidecar.
- **Sting / wordmark close.** End with the WGU wordmark (no owl) or the full university logo.
- **Length-aware copy.** 25 word maximum for a 15-second spot.

## Print collateral (one-pagers, brochures)

- **Type.** Real Futura PT (licensed) or Arial fallback.
- **Color.** Pantone references where exact match matters. Ask Brand Governance for current PMS values.
- **Logo.** Minimum 0.725 inch wide.
- **Imagery.** 300dpi minimum.
- **Patterns.** One pattern per piece, low-contrast.

## Apparel and swag

- **Approval required.** Brand Governance reviews all apparel and swag before production.
- **Owl icon** acceptable on student, alumni, and employee swag.
- **Academic Seal** is not a swag mark unless explicitly approved.
- **Color matching.** Pantone references mandatory for printed apparel.

## In all channels

- WGU Blue (#002855) appears on every asset.
- Logos and marks unmodified.
- Voice characteristics intact.
- No owl puns.
- No emoji in brand collateral.
