# WGU Brand and Design System Knowledge Pack

This is the complete knowledge pack for **Western Governors University (WGU)** brand and design system. Upload all files in this directory to Claude's ask-your-org tool so anyone at WGU can ask questions like "what color is WGU blue?", "how do I use the owl icon?", or "what fonts are approved?".

## How to use this pack

In Claude.ai, open Settings -> Knowledge (or "Ask your org") and upload all `.md` files in this folder. Once indexed, your team can ask any question and Claude will cite the right file.

## Source of truth

These files distill the official WGU Brand Guide at https://www.brand.wgu.edu/ plus the Evergreen presentation template. The official assets, full icon library, and licensed fonts live in the WGU DAM (Canto). For anything not covered here, contact **brand.wgu.edu**.
