# WGU Logos and Marks

## The four marks

WGU has four distinct marks. Each has a specific use case and clear-space rule.

1. **University logo (master mark).** The owl figure with the "Western Governors University" wordmark beneath it. Use for public-facing institutional communications.
2. **Owl icon.** The owl figure alone, no wordmark. Use only for student, alumni, or employee audiences.
3. **WGU wordmark.** The "Western Governors University" wordmark alone, no owl. Use when the audience already knows the brand and a smaller, text-only mark is appropriate.
4. **Academic Seal.** The formal university seal. Restricted to official documents (diplomas, transcripts, formal letters from the President). Apparel or swag uses must be cleared with Brand Governance.

## Two separate marks rule

The wordmark and the owl icon are **two separate marks**. They are not used together as a custom lockup. If you want both the owl and the words "Western Governors University" in one place, use the official university logo, do not assemble your own.

## Clear space

- **Master mark (university logo) clear space.** Minimum clear space on all four sides equals one half the X-height of the master mark. Nothing intrudes inside that buffer.
- **Owl icon clear space.** Minimum clear space equals one third the icon height on all four sides.

## Minimum size

- Web. **52 px wide** minimum for the master mark.
- Print. **0.725 inch wide** minimum for the master mark.

Going below these sizes degrades the mark and reduces brand recall.

## Usage rules

- Never recolor the logo. Use only the approved color versions (Blue Midnight on light, white on dark).
- Never rotate or angle the logo.
- Never place the logo over a busy photograph without a protection gradient or solid backing.
- Never modify the proportions, stretch, or condense.
- Never animate the logo unless using an officially supplied animated asset.
- Never combine with other organizations' logos in a sandwich lockup without Brand Governance approval.

## Co-branding and partnerships

Co-branded marks (WGU plus partner) require Brand Governance review. Email brand.wgu.edu before producing co-branded assets.

## The owl: when and where

| Audience | Approved mark |
| --- | --- |
| Prospective students | University logo |
| Current students | University logo or owl icon |
| Alumni | University logo or owl icon |
| Employees and faculty | University logo or owl icon |
| General public, press | University logo |
| Employers and B2B | University logo |
| Official documents | Academic Seal plus university logo |

## What not to do with the owl

- No owl puns in copy that accompanies the owl mark.
- No anthropomorphizing the owl ("the owl is here to help").
- No naming the owl or referring to it as a character. Sage is the costumed mascot, separate from the icon.
- No rotating or animating the static owl mark in user-generated material.
