The WGU difference

A different model for how college works.

Traditional

  • Rigid semester schedule
  • Credit-hour tuition, variable cost
  • Lectures at set times
  • One-size-fits-all pacing

WGU

  • Start any month, test when ready
  • Flat-rate tuition per 6-month term
  • 24/7 access to course materials
  • Competency-based — move at your pace
